Global Markets Brace for a Volatile Quarter Ahead

By Editorial Desk · December 2025

Investors entered the final quarter of the year confronting a volatile mix of inflationary pressure, geopolitical uncertainty, and tightening monetary policy across major economies.

Analysts warn that while equity markets have shown resilience, underlying fundamentals suggest caution rather than confidence should guide capital allocation strategies.

The coming months are expected to test not only portfolios, but the broader assumptions that have driven market optimism since the post-pandemic recovery.
